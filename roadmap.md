## Operating principles

1. **Ship something small every week.** A note, a script, a summary — but something dated.
2. **No empty scaffolding.** A folder appears when there's real work to put in it.
3. **Document the thinking, not just the result.** The reasoning is the portfolio.
4. **Reps over inspiration.** Do the boring thing on schedule. The myelin is built by repetition, not motivation.
